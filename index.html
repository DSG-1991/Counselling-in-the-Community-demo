Counselling in the Community

Affordable, compassionate counselling for everyone in Blackpool and beyond

Our Mission

We are a registered charity providing accessible, affordable mental health support for children (4+), young people, and adults. With more than 130 volunteers across four hubs, we support around 150 people each week.

Session Fees

  • Initial Assessment – £10
  • Unemployed / Benefits – £5
  • Income up to £20,000 – £10
  • Income £20,001–£30,000 – £20
  • Income £30,001+ – £30

No one is ever turned away for financial reasons.
