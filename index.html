How We Help

Counselling for Everyone

Professional, person-centred counselling for people of all ages — in person, online, or by telephone.

🤺

Children (Age 4–12)

Gentle, play-based therapeutic support in a safe, nurturing environment — including our specialist sensory room in Bispham.

🧑

Young People (13–17)

Confidential support for teenagers navigating anxiety, depression, school pressure, and life challenges — on their terms.

🧠

Adults

One-to-one counselling for adults facing grief, anxiety, depression, addiction, relationship difficulties, and beyond.

💻

Online & Telephone

Access our full service from home via secure video call or telephone — the same quality of care, wherever you are.

Counselling in the Community

Affordable, compassionate support for everyone in Blackpool and beyond

Our Mission

We are a registered charity providing accessible, affordable mental health support for children (aged 4+), young people, and adults. With more than 130 volunteers across four hubs, we support around 150 people every week.

No matter your background or circumstances, we believe everyone deserves access to quality counselling.

Session Fees

Our sliding scale ensures cost is never a barrier to getting support.

  • Initial Assessment £10
  • Unemployed / Benefits £5
  • Income up to £20,000 £10
  • Income £20,001–£30,000 £20
  • Income £30,001+ £30

No one is ever turned away for financial reasons.
